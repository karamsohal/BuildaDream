Dreamer Day Fest

October 22 @ 9:30 AM - 2:00 PM

Enercare Centre: 1-100 Princes' Blvd, Toronto, ON, Canada

DREAM BIG at our 3rd Annual Dreamer Day

Dreamer Day Fest celebrates female empowerment, originating from the UN’s International Day of the Girl. Now a vibrant festival at Toronto's Enercare Centre, it empowers female students in trades, STEAM, emergency response, entrepreneurship, and leadership.

Join us to explore interactive exhibitor booths, career opportunities, and industry insights. Enjoy dynamic keynotes, engaging panels, and live entertainment. Capture memories at our Instagram-worthy photo stations and ignite your passion for the future!

You're Hired

October 23 @ 11:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Enercare Centre: 1-100 Princes' Blvd, Toronto, ON, Canada

Unlock Your Future: A Gateway to the Skilled Trades

Join us at the Enercare Centre in Toronto to explore opportunities in the skilled trades sector. Connect with leading companies, including energy firms and manufacturers, ask questions, and meet potential employers face-to-face. This event offers valuable insights and networking opportunities for women in skilled trades. Tickets are free for job seekers, with 3,000 available. Don’t miss this chance to advance your career!
