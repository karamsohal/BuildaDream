Student Career Expo

October 22 @ 9:30 AM - 2:00 PM

Enercare Centre: 1-100 Princes' Blvd, Toronto, ON, Canada

Dreamer Day is the ultimate festival for women identifying students looking to explore career opportunities in traditionally male dominated fields such as the skilled trades, STEAM and more! Complete with a live DJ, glow sticks, photobooths, VR (Virtual Reality) simulators, epic vibes and more; this year’s event will see 3,000+ students from various educational institutions get inspired, meet with representatives from top companies, attend workshops, and learn to dream big!

Job Fair

October 23 @ 11:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Enercare Centre: 1-100 Princes' Blvd, Toronto, ON, Canada

More than a traditional job fair, You’re Hired targets job seekers of all ages and experience levels looking to begin or pivot to careers skilled trades and STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics). Attendees will have the ability to match and meet with recruiters, get meaningful feedback on their skills, and attend a clinic that will allow them to learn more about apprenticeship, upskilling opportunities, and get on-the-spot guidance from career counselors.
